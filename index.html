Cleaning the Tank

Performing Regular Maintenance

  1. Remove uneaten food
  2. Rinse the collection cup
  3. Wipe away any salt creep you see

Following a Biweekly Cleaning Schedule

  1. Remove 25% of the water
  2. Clean the glass panes of the tank
  3. Vacuum the substrate
  4. Wipe the protein skimmer
  5. Rinse the prefilters
  6. Add new salt water to the tank

Following a Long-Term Cleaning Schedule

  1. Clean the aquarium and lighting fixture covers
  2. Clean the air intake hose and skimmer
  3. Deep clean the aquarium every few months
